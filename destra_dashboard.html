About DesTra

"Your Journey, Your Budget, Your Control"

What is DesTra?

DesTra is a smart travel expense management platform designed for modern travellers. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway to Goa or an international trip to Singapore, DesTra helps you track, calculate, and manage every single expense, so you always know exactly where your money is going.

Why DesTra?

Most travellers lose track of their spending mid-trip. They overspend on food, forget to account for local transport, or get surprised by hotel charges. DesTra was built to solve this exact problem, giving you a clear, organized view of your travel budget before, during, and after your trip.

What can you do with DesTra?

🗺️
Trip Planning
Enter source and destination, see your route on a real interactive map.
💸
Expense Tracking
Track travel, food, hotel, shopping, and return expenses separately.
📊
Visual Breakdown
Pie and bar charts show exactly where your money goes.
💱
Currency Converter
Convert between 14+ world currencies instantly.
📋
Trip History
Save and review daily expenses for multi-day trips with overall totals.
🔐
Secure Login
Firebase authentication with Google sign-in keeps your data safe.

Our Mission

We believe every traveller deserves to experience the world without financial stress. DesTra's mission is simple, make travel budgeting so easy that anyone, anywhere can plan a trip with complete confidence. Travel more, worry less.

Built with
